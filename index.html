The Moharana Legacy

For centuries, the Moharana community has held the sacred chisel. We are the traditional architects responsible for constructing the chariots of the Rath Yatra in Puri.


Every piece in our store is carved from sacred wood (Neem, Sal) or stone, carrying the blessings of Lord Jagannath and the touch of generations of mastery.


"By the grace of the Lord, our hands shape faith."
